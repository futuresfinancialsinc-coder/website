Start a Chapter

Bring Futures Financials to your community

A chapter is a local branch of our nonprofit, run by students, dedicated to expanding financial literacy where you live.

What is a chapter

Local branches, one global mission

Chapters range from 6 to 50+ members and can be established as school clubs, community organizations, or regional initiatives, bringing financial education to students through workshops, events, and outreach programs.

Wherever a chapter starts, it plugs into a worldwide network of 18 chapters across 7 countries and 450+ volunteers.

The process

How to launch your chapter

1

Prepare your foundation

If your chapter will be based at a school, communicate with your school administration and obtain preliminary approval before submitting your application.

2

Submit your application

Complete the Futures Financials Chapter Application and receive an update within one week of submission. Due to the volume of applications, not all submissions can be accepted.

3

Interview with our team

If selected to advance, you'll schedule an interview to discuss your chapter goals, demonstrate your commitment to financial literacy, and share your vision for impact in your community.
