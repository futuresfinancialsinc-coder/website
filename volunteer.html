Volunteer

Volunteers are the heart of everything we do

Join a global community of students turning financial knowledge into real impact for their peers.

How it works

Three steps to start making an impact

1

Choose your team

Pick one of our two volunteer tracks below and submit the online application.

2

Complete your onboarding

Once accepted, you'll get access to our volunteer platform, training resources, and chapter channels, and meet your team lead.

3

Start making an impact

Lead workshops, build content, and organize events. Eligible activities count toward community service hours and leadership recognition.

Choose your team

Two ways to get involved

Track 01

Education Team

Help teach financial literacy by leading workshops, creating educational presentations, mentoring students, and developing engaging learning materials on topics like budgeting, investing, credit, entrepreneurship, and economics.

Apply Now
Track 02

Outreach & Partnerships Team

Expand our impact by organizing community events, building partnerships with schools and organizations, managing social media campaigns, and supporting outreach initiatives that make financial education accessible to more students.
