About Us

The magic of young people

At Futures Financials, we believe in the extraordinary power of youth to create meaningful change.

We're a global movement that challenges conventional thinking about who can lead, who can learn, and how we support young people in building lifelong financial literacy.

"Financial confidence starts with financial knowledge."

Community at its core

Financial education that meets students where they are

Our global community model ensures that financial education is accessible, engaging, and culturally relevant for students from every background.

It also means that our volunteers are constantly learning from one another, growing not just as educators and mentors, but as financially empowered global citizens committed to expanding economic opportunity.

The ripple effect

Every lesson sends ripples far beyond the classroom

Every student we educate, every volunteer we empower, and every financial barrier we break creates ripples that extend far beyond our immediate community.

Why, what & how

What Futures Financials stands for

Our why

Why we exist

Founded in 2022, Futures Financials was created to reimagine how young people learn about money, taking a practical, real-world approach and proving that students have the power to build financially confident futures.

Our what

What we do

We work to make financial literacy engaging, accessible, and practical by fostering a supportive learning community. From interactive workshops and educational resources to mentorship and real-world experiences, Futures Financials delivers financial education in a way that resonates with young people.

Our how

How we do it

As a global nonprofit dedicated to financial literacy, we bring together more than 450 student volunteers across 7 countries. Through 18 Futures Financials chapters, we empower young people to develop financial confidence, leadership, and real-world skills.
