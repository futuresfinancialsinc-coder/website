Our Programs

Futures Financials operates three flagship financial literacy programs that transform students from passive learners into financially confident decision-makers through experiential, personalized, and community-driven education.

How We Teach

We've sat in your seats

As students ourselves, we had to sit through lectures from guest speakers, so we know firsthand how boring they can be. At Futures Financials, we bring that experience in the audience to our teaching by making every program highly interactive and engaging the audience whenever possible.

Interactive

Questions throughout every session that keep the audience involved.

Relatable

Gen Alpha / Z terminology and references that meet students where they are.

Competitive

Games at the end of sessions to spark a positive, competitive spirit within students.

The curriculums

Three examples, one clear progression

The three presentations below are just a few examples of lessons we've delivered before. Read together, they show how our curriculum builds across grade levels, starting with the basics in middle school and advancing to real-world money skills by high school.

Real-World Money Skills
